## What is FitGuideAI?

FitGuideAI is an iOS app that generates personalized workout programs using AI and exercise science. You answer questions about your goals, experience level, available equipment, and schedule — and FitGuideAI builds a complete training program tailored to you.

## How does it work?

During onboarding, you tell us about:
- Your training experience and fitness goals
- How many days per week you can train
- What equipment you have access to
- Which muscle groups you want to prioritize

Our AI engine uses this information alongside established exercise science principles (volume landmarks, progressive overload, movement patterns) to generate a structured program with appropriate exercises, sets, reps, and progression schemes.

## Is FitGuideAI free?

Yes. FitGuideAI is completely free with no subscriptions, paywalls, or in-app purchases.

## What platforms is it available on?

FitGuideAI is currently being developed for iOS (iPhone). An Android version may follow in the future.

## Can I customize my program?

Absolutely. While AI generates your starting program, you have full control to:
- Swap any exercise for an alternative
- Adjust sets, reps, and weights
- Add or remove exercises
- Reorganize your training days

## What is Smart Progression?

Smart Progression automatically tracks your performance and suggests weight increases when you're ready. It also detects plateaus, schedules deload weeks, and manages block periodization — so your training evolves with you.

## What is Gym Mode?

Gym Mode is a step-by-step workout interface that guides you through each set during your session. It includes rest timers, a plate calculator (tells you exactly which plates to load), warm-up set suggestions, and real-time tracking.

## What equipment does it support?

FitGuideAI supports:
- Full commercial gym setups
- Home gyms with barbells and dumbbells
- Minimal equipment (dumbbells only, resistance bands)
- Bodyweight-only training

## How is my data stored?

Your profile and workout data are stored securely using AWS cloud services (Cognito for authentication, DynamoDB for data). We don't sell or share your personal information. See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) for details.
