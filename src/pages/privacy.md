## Introduction

FitGuideAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the FitGuideAI mobile application ("the App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
When you create an account and use the App, we collect:
- **Account information**: Email address and password (password is hashed and stored securely via AWS Cognito)
- **Profile information**: Age, gender, height, weight, training experience level, fitness goals, and equipment availability
- **Workout data**: Programs, exercises, sets, reps, weights, and workout history

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device information**: Device model, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage data**: App feature usage and crash reports (for improving the App)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Generate personalized workout programs
- Track your workout progress and provide smart progression recommendations
- Improve the App and fix bugs
- Communicate with you about the App (e.g., support responses)

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored using **AWS cloud services** (Amazon Cognito for authentication, Amazon DynamoDB for data storage) in the US-West-2 (Oregon) region
- All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms via AWS Cognito
- We follow security best practices to protect your data

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties** for marketing purposes.

We may share information only in these circumstances:
- **Service providers**: AWS (cloud infrastructure) — they process data on our behalf under strict data protection agreements
- **Legal requirements**: If required by law, regulation, or legal process
- **Safety**: To protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or the public

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data by viewing your profile in the App
- **Update** your information at any time through the App
- **Delete** your account and all associated data by contacting us at [help@fitguideai.com](mailto:help@fitguideai.com)
- **Export** your workout data (feature coming soon)

## Data Retention

- We retain your data for as long as your account is active
- If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days
- Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes

## Children's Privacy

FitGuideAI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, if significant, through an in-app notification.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
- Email: [help@fitguideai.com](mailto:help@fitguideai.com)
